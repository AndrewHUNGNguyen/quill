Skills & Accomplishments

Before the hackathon, all participants must update their profile on ohack.dev/profile. This is a crucial step to ensure we can match you effectively with projects and team members. Please make sure to:

  1. Visit ohack.dev/profile and log in to your account.
  2. Update your skills, experience, and areas of expertise.
  3. Upload a current resume or skill sheet (PDF format preferred).

Your profile should include:

  • Technical skills and proficiency levels
  • Relevant experience (professional, academic, or personal projects)
  • Areas of interest within technology and social impact
  • Any specific skills or experience you'd like to share with potential team members

Completing this step is mandatory for participation in the hackathon. It helps us create balanced teams and allows other participants to understand your background and strengths.

Remember: A comprehensive profile increases your chances of finding the perfect project match and showcases your abilities to potential teammates and mentors.



Travel

We want to make it as easy as possible for you to join us at Opportunity Hack Arizona! Our reimbursement policy is designed to help offset your travel costs:

  • Up to $25 for travel within Arizona
  • Up to $150 for travel from neighboring states (California, Nevada, Utah, Colorado, New Mexico)
  • Up to $200 for travel from elsewhere in the United States
  • Up to $300 for international travel

Please note:

  1. We only reimburse the minimum-cost travel path. This means using the most economical mode of transportation available.
  2. If you're driving from within Arizona or neighboring states, we encourage carpooling. Ask in Slack in #travel to help you connect with other participants for ride-sharing.
  3. For those flying in, we recommend flying into Phoenix Sky Harbor International Airport (PHX).
  4. All reimbursements will be processed after the hackathon. You'll need to submit receipts and follow our reimbursement procedure, which will be explained during the event.
  5. If you require special accommodations or have questions about travel arrangements, please contact our team in our #travel Slack channel

For full details on our travel policy, eligibility requirements, and reimbursement process, please visit your participant dashboard.

We're excited to welcome you to Arizona for an amazing hackathon experience!
